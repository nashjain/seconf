SeConf 2014

4th Annual Selenium Conference

Sep 4-6th 2014, Bangalore

The Selenium Conference is a volunteer-run, non-profit event presented by members of the Selenium Community. The goal of the conference is to bring together Selenium developers & enthusiasts from around the world to share ideas, socialize, and work together on advancing the present and future success of the project.

This year, the conference is hosted by AgileFAQs in conjunction with the SeConf Organising Committee.

The conference begins Thursday with all-day workshops, followed by two days of presentations by experts and practitioners from around the world. The conference will host 2 parallel tracks. There will also be pair-coding sessions throughout the day, as well as opportunities to get Selenium advice and tutorials from experts.

Speakers

Call for Proposals

June 30th was the last day to submit your talk proposals for SeConf14. The presenters have time until July 6th, to update their proposals based on public feedback they receive in the submission system.

REVIEW PROCESS

On July 7th, the submission system will be locked and the core-review team will review all the proposals. We are hoping that by July 18th we should be able to announce a tentative program.

All proposals will be public, however the identity of the proposal submitter will not be revealed. This is to encourage DIVERSITY and to ensure that the selection committee is not biased.

Registered user of the submission system will be able to comment on the proposals. Submitters may also post comments on reviews or public comments of their own proposals to provide clarifications, explain revisions and respond to questions. Again, the comments do not reveal the identity of the person commenting. However the public comments are inputs, which can be utilized by both the submitter and the review team. Ultimately the decision to accept a session resides with the program committee.

Your proposal stands the best chance to be selected, if:

  • * the topic is unique and compelling,
  • * the abstract clearly describes the essence of your session,
  • * you've selected the right theme/category...
    (Selenium Internals, Selenium for Mobile, Best Practices or Infrastructure)
  • * your session duration, level and session type is selected appropriately
  • * your proposal clearly highlights the learning outcomes for the attendees
  • * your proposal shows a clear breakup of your session timing

We strongly discourage tool authors from hijacking the conference to pitch their products.

Your tool (commercial or open-source) might have solved a hard problem, that the entire community is facing. We would love to hear about the problem and your technical solution. However please make sure you don't turn this into a marketing talk.

Compensation for speakers

Speakers will be compensated as follows:

60 or 90 minutes session 1 Free Conference Registration + 2 Hotel Night
45 minutes session 1 Free Conference Registration + 1 Hotel Night
< 45 minutes session 1 Free Conference Registration
< 20 minutes No Compensation

Speakers can contact sales@agilefaqs.com for discount code to register for the conference.

Compensation listed above is only for primary speakers. Co-presenters can avail a 25% discount on their registrations. Hotel nights are capped at 3 nights for speakers with multiple accepted sessions. They cannot be accumulated beyond the cap or transferred to another person. Free registrations cannot be accumulated or transferred.

VISA for Entering India

We recommend all foreign delegates (speakers & participants) apply for a tourist visa. This is the safest, cheapest and most hassle-free visa. All you need is your confirmed flight tickets and hotel confirmation to apply for this visa.

Current citizens of Cambodia, Finland, Japan, Laos, Luxembourg, Myanmar, New Zealand, Philippines, Singapore, and Vietnam can get a "Tourist Visa On Arrival". Its valid for 30 days, for single entry and is available only at the international airports at Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore and Kolkata. Citizens from other countries are required to apply for the visa before hand. Give at least 3 weeks for the visa process. Some agencies that can help you

  • US - http://www.traveldocs.com/index.php?page=india
  • UK - http://in.vfsglobal.co.uk/
